Larger Rooms

Our hotel has 16 rooms - 12 double rooms and 4 studios. Each of them consists of a living room, bedroom and a large bathroom.

Conference room

The conference room is and excellent place for seminars, business meetings and training. It has a capacity of around 30 people, depending of arrangement and type of activity.

Lobby bar

The nice atmosphere and great drinks make the lobby bar a perfect place for business or casual meetings.

In the center of Sofia

Situated on 135 Todor Aleksandrov boulevard, its only 1.5km away from the "Largo" and 50m away from metro station "Konstantin Velichkov", which has a direct line to Sofia Airport.

Central West Hotel

"Central West Hotel" is a new hotel which has opened in the center of Sofia in 2018. Located in a great part of the city, you can find numerous restaurants, bars, shops and business locations near us. Our hotel has 12 spacious double rooms as well as 4 large studios and all of them are well decorated and include a living room, bedroom and a large bathroom.
