About Us

“Central West Hotel” is the new hotel in the center of the city of Sofia. Our rooms are so spacious (35 sq.m. average) and comfortable that you will quickly feel like at home. The hotel staff will also help with this, since for us it is something much more than a workplace. We have at your disposal 16 rooms - 4 apartments and 12 double rooms. Each of them consists of a living room, bedroom and bathroom and is equipped with comfortable furniture, which will benefit both your working, as well as your holiday. The lobby bar of the hotel is the ideal place for meetings, working online or just to enjoy a high quality beverage with a friend in a pleasant environment. We also have a fully equipped conference room available for rent which is spacious enough for up to 45 people and is suitable for all sorts of events.

How to reach us

You can find us at 135 Todor Aleksandrov boulevard. If you are arriving with a car - we have parking available, if not - just 50 metres from the hotel is the metro station "Konstantin Velichkov". You can arrive from the airport to our location in only 20 minutes, without changing lanes. We hope to see you soon!

Central West Hotel

"Central West Hotel" is a new hotel which has opened in the center of Sofia in 2018. Located in a great part of the city, you can find numerous restaurants, bars, shops and business locations near us. Our hotel has 12 spacious double rooms as well as 4 large apartments and all of them are well decorated and include a living room, bedroom and a large bathroom.
