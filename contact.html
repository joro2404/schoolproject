About Us

“Central West Hotel” is the new hotel in the centre of the city of Sofia. Our rooms are so spacious (35 sq.m. average) and cozy that you will quickly feel like at home. Our hotel team will also help with that, since for us it is something much more than a workplace. We have at your disposal 16 rooms - 4 apartments and 12 double rooms. Each of them consists of a living room, bedroom and bathroom and is equipped with comfortable furniture, which will benefit both your working, as well as your holiday. The lobby bar of the hotel is the ideal place for meetings, working online or just to enjoy a high quality beverage in a pleasant environment.

How to reach us

You can find us at 135 Todor Aleksandrov boulevard. If you are arriving with a car - we have parking available, if not - just 50 metres from the hotel is the metrostation "Konstantin Velichkov". You can arrive from the airport to our location in only 20 minutes, without changing lanes. See you soon!
